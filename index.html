Archcraft

Yet another minimal Linux distribution, based on Arch Linux.

Minimal

Archcraft is very minimal and lightweight. It can run under 500Mb of memory as It only uses window managers and lightweight applications.

Stunning

Flat and pastel colors, the consistency among all the UI elements, And various themes make Archcraft look very clean, minimal and Stunning.

Powerful

With built-in support for AUR, Chaotic AUR and being lightweight, Archcraft gives you the power to do whatever you want on your Linux PC.
